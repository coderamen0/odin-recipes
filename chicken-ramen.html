Chicken Ramen


Image credits:Michele Blackwell

Description:

Chicken Ramen is a delectable Japanese noodle soup that combines tender pieces of chicken with perfectly cooked ramen noodles, all swimming in a flavorful broth. This comforting dish offers a harmonious blend of savory and umami notes, creating a satisfying and heartwarming culinary experience. Topped with fresh vegetables and a sprinkling of green onions, Chicken Ramen is a delightful balance of textures and tastes, making it a go-to comfort food for any occasion. Prepare to immerse yourself in a bowl of warmth and deliciousness with this classic and comforting recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: soft-boiled egg, nori seaweed, chili oil

Steps:

  • Cook Chicken: Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a pan. Season chicken with salt and pepper, then sear until fully cooked. Slice into thin strips.
  • Prepare Noodles: Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • Make Broth: In a pot, saut√© grated ginger and minced garlic in 1 tablespoon vegetable oil. Add chicken broth and soy sauce. Bring to a simmer.
  • Assemble Ramen: Add sliced mushrooms to the broth and cook until tender. Stir in sesame oil and spinach until wilted. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  • Serve: Divide cooked noodles among bowls. Ladle hot broth over noodles. Top with sliced chicken, green onions, and any optional toppings you desire.
  • Enjoy: Serve hot and savor the rich flavors of your homemade Chicken Ramen!
